Getting There

Arriving at Elland Road by Public Transport

National Rail

Leeds Railway Station [LDS]
Address: New Station Street, Leeds, LS1 4DY
Distance: 2 miles from stadium
(See directions in Bus section below)

Leeds is well connected to the MetroTrain network with regular services from all major cities. The following services operate from Leeds Station:

  • East Coast Main Line → London King's Cross
  • Midland Main Line → London St Pancras
  • Airedale Line → Keighley, Skipton, Heysham Port, Morecambe, Carlisle
  • Caldervale Line → Manchester Oxford Road, Blackpool North, Huddersfield
  • Hallam Line → Castleford, Wakefield, Barnsley, Sheffield
  • Harrogate Line → Harrogate, Knaresborough, York
  • Huddersfield Line → Dewsbury, Huddersfield
  • Pontefract Line → Castleford, Pontefract, Knottingley, Goole
  • Wakefield Line → Wakefield, Doncaster, Sheffield
  • Wharfedale Line → Ilkley
  • Leeds–Bradford Lines → Bradford

Bus

A shuttle bus service (R2) operates on matchdays, run by First Leeds Buses. The service departs from Sovereign Street, which is located near the railway station.

The shuttle service begins 90 minutes before kick-off and runs at frequent intervals. A return service operates after the match.

Pricing:

  • £3 return (adults)
  • £1.50 (children/OAPs)
  • £2 single (adults)
  • £1 single (children/OAPs)

Regular bus services 51, 52, and 55 also run from Leeds city centre to Elland Road. Please note that bus stops in the immediate vicinity of the stadium are suspended for one hour before and after the match.

Arriving at Elland Road by Car

Elland Road is easily accessible from the M1, M62, and M621 motorways. Click here for Google Maps directions.

From the North

Take the A1, M1, and A639 towards Leeds. Join the M621 and exit at Junction 1. Follow the A6110 to Elland Road.

From the South

Take the M1 to the M621 and exit at Junction 1. Follow the A6110 to Elland Road.

From the East

Take the A64 and A1, then follow the same route as from the North. Alternatively, take the M62 to the M621 and exit at Junction 1.

From the West

Take the M62 to the M621 and exit at Junction 1. Follow the A6110 to Elland Road.

Postcode: LS11 0ES

Parking: Leeds City Council operates several car parks around the stadium. Pricing is as follows:

  • Cars → £6
  • Mini coaches → £12
  • Coaches → £20

Street parking in the surrounding area is restricted to resident permit holders only. Disabled parking is available for Blue Badge holders.

Contact:
Phone: 0113 395 7400
Email: parks@leeds.gov.uk

Leeds Ticket Information

Tickets for Premier League matches at Elland Road for Leeds fans are generally prioritised to members.

Members have an exclusive period to book ahead of general sale. Gold Members have first priority. All tickets subject to availability.

Note: A membership does not guarantee a ticket.
